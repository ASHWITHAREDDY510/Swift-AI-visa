## United States - Student Visa
### Source: United_States_Student.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Enrolled in SEVP-certified school
- Have Form I-20 from school
- Prove sufficient funds for study
- Maintain full-time student status

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Form I-20 from school
- SEVIS fee payment receipt
- DS-160 confirmation page
- Financial evidence

PROCESSING TIME: 1-3 months
FEES: $160 (SEVIS fee: $350)
