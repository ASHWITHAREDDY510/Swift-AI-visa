## United Kingdom - Tourist Visa
### Source: United_Kingdom_Tourist.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Intend to leave UK at end of visit
- Not live in UK for extended periods
- Have sufficient funds for stay
- Not work or study without authorization

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Bank statements (6 months)
- Employment letter
- Travel itinerary
- Return flight tickets

PROCESSING TIME: 3-8 weeks
FEES: £100
