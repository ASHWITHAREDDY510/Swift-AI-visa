## United Kingdom - Student Visa
### Source: United_Kingdom_Student.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Unconditional offer from licensed student sponsor
- Prove English language proficiency
- Show sufficient funds for course fees and living costs
- Be from country requiring visa

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS)
- Proof of financial support (£1,334/month for London)
- English language test results
- TB test certificate (if applicable)

PROCESSING TIME: 3-8 weeks
FEES: £490
