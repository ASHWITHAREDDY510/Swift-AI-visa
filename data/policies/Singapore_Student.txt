## Singapore - Student Visa
### Source: Singapore_Student.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Acceptance from approved Singapore institution
- Meet academic requirements
- Prove sufficient funds for study
- Attend full-time course

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Letter of acceptance
- Academic transcripts
- Financial evidence
- Passport-sized photographs

PROCESSING TIME: 2-4 weeks
FEES: SGD $30-90
