## Canada - Tourist Visa
### Source: Canada_Tourist.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Have valid travel document
- Be in good health
- No criminal or immigration convictions
- Convince officer you'll leave Canada
- Have sufficient funds for stay

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Bank statements (6 months)
- Employment letter
- Travel itinerary
- Proof of ties to home country

PROCESSING TIME: 2-8 weeks
FEES: CAD $100
