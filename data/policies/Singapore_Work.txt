## Singapore - Work Visa
### Source: Singapore_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Job offer from Singapore employer
- Meet minimum salary requirements
- Have acceptable qualifications
- Employer must apply on your behalf

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Educational certificates
- Employment letter
- Passport-sized photographs
- Medical report (if required)

PROCESSING TIME: 3-8 weeks
FEES: SGD $105-225
