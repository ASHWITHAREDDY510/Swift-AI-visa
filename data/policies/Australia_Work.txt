## Australia - Work Visa
### Source: Australia_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Have skilled occupation on relevant list
- Meet points test requirements
- Have positive skills assessment
- Meet health and character requirements

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Skills assessment
- English language test results
- Educational certificates
- Work experience letters

PROCESSING TIME: 6-12 months
FEES: AUD $4,045-7,320
