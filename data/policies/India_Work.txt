## India - Work Visa
### Source: India_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Employment offer from Indian company
- Meet minimum salary threshold
- Have relevant qualifications and experience
- Employer must be registered in India

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport (6+ months)
- Employment contract
- Educational certificates
- Professional qualifications proof
- Police clearance certificate

PROCESSING TIME: 2-4 weeks
FEES: Varies by nationality
