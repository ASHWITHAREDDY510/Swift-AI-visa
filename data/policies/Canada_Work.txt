## Canada - Work Visa
### Source: Canada_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Valid job offer from Canadian employer
- Employer may need LMIA
- Prove you'll leave Canada when permit expires
- Show sufficient funds for stay

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Job offer letter
- LMIA (if required)
- Proof of qualifications
- Medical exam results

PROCESSING TIME: 1-9 months
FEES: CAD $155-255
