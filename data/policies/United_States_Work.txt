## United States - Work Visa
### Source: United_States_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Job offer from US employer
- Employer must file petition (Form I-129)
- Meet visa category requirements (H-1B, L-1, etc.)
- Prove qualifications for position

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Approved petition (Form I-797)
- Educational certificates
- Resume/CV
- DS-160 confirmation page

PROCESSING TIME: 2-6 months
FEES: $190-460
