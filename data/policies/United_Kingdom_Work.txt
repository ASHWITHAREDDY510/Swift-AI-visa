## United Kingdom - Work Visa
### Source: United_Kingdom_Work.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Work for a UK employer approved by Home Office
- Have 'certificate of sponsorship' (CoS) from employer
- Do a job on eligible occupations list
- Be paid minimum salary (varies by occupation)
- Prove knowledge of English language

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport or travel document
- Certificate of Sponsorship reference number
- Proof of English language (IELTS/TOEFL)
- Bank statements showing financial support
- TB test certificate (if applicable)

PROCESSING TIME: 3-8 weeks
FEES: £719-£1,500
