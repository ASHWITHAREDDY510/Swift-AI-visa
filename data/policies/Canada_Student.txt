## Canada - Student Visa
### Source: Canada_Student.txt

REQUIREMENTS:
- Acceptance letter from designated learning institution
- Prove sufficient funds for tuition and living
- Be law-abiding citizen with no criminal record
- Be in good health

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport
- Letter of acceptance
- Proof of financial support (CAD $10,000+/year)
- Immigration medical exam
- Police clearance certificate

PROCESSING TIME: 4-12 weeks
FEES: CAD $150
